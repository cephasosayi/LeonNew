About Leon Football Academy

Leon football Academy is a Nigerian-owned football academy established to provide a conducive environment for the growth and development of young players, as well as rendering financial assistance to every young footballer who aspires to play at a professional level in Nigeria and beyond.

There are different independent academies in Nigeria, but none caters to their players’ welfare as well as utilize a fair percentage system like we do. The Academy also looks to provide assistance to other individuals in need of other services in this field.

Mission

Our Mission is to produce the next generation of big stars and talents in the world of football and give them the best education of the game that will last them for the rest of their lives.

vision

Our Vision is to become the biggest football academy in Nigeria and Africa, to have branches and centers in every part of the world, to become the most successful football academy with the type of players that will be produced for National teams and football world.

Our Academy

The Academy provides a safe and enjoyable environment for children to participate in football related activities without any cultural, sexual or social prejudice. We at Leon Football Academy encourage equal participation regardless of skill level.The Academy is committed to an environment where opportunities are given to everyone at equal measure with no favoritism.

The Academy is committed to prohibiting victimization or discrimination of players and spectators on the basis of their race or religion. Leon Football Academy is responsible for youth and will conduct activities in a manner that is in keeping with the plans and wishes of the reserved Nation.

The Academy will be financed by herself. If necessary, sponsors may be sought after from responsible businesses and legitimate institutions. The Academy values its sponsors and in return is obliged to provide fair and reasonable publicity advertisment to the sponsor in any manner that is mutually agreed.Game days will be a community event which will have family atmosphere, with organized, coordinated and professional presentation of our team.

The Academy at all levels show courtesy and respect to our opponents and is always gracious in both victory and defeat. Opposing teams will received by a warm welcome to our hospitable environment. They will be treated not just as opponents, but also as our partners who have contributed to the provision of a safe and constructive competition for our children.

Our Strategy

Our Strategyaim is to provide the best of facilities, coaching and games programme to create a perfect environment for players to develop and reach their full potentials. We aim to develop players to play in the first team of different clubs in every part of the world as professionals and our admission process is rigorous and on a continuous basis as more talented qualified students are discovered and invited to join the academy.

We aim to have a 120 student’s capacity hostel for the junior and senior teams in the nearest future where they will be 3 coaches for both levels.
